The Tudor Dynasty

"This is the list of six Tudor Monarchs with their nicknames for 118 years from 1485 to 1603."

  1. Henry VII - "The Huckster King"
  2. Henry VIII - "Coopernose"
  3. Edward VI - "The Boy King"
  4. Lady Jane Grey - "The Nine Days' Queen"
  5. Mary I - "Bloody Mary"
  6. Elizabeth I - "The Virgin Queen"
